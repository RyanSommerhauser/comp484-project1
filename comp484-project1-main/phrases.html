  • Phrases

  • こんにちはKonnichiwa

    • Romanization: Konnichiwa
    • Pronunciation: Kon-ni-chi-wa
    • Translation: Hello / Good afternoon
    • Usage: Common greeting during daytime, suitable for meeting people in Japan.

  • ありがとうArigatō

    • Romanization: Arigatō
    • Pronunciation: A-ri-ga-toh
    • Translation: Thank you
    • Usage: Expressing gratitude in most situations.

  • すみませんSumimasen

    • Romanization: Sumimasen
    • Pronunciation: Su-mi-ma-sen
    • Translation: Excuse me / I'm sorry
    • Usage: Can be used to get someone's attention or to apologize.

  • はいHai

    • Romanization: Hai
    • Pronunciation: Hai
    • Translation: Yes
    • Usage: Affirmative response.

  • いいえIie

    • Romanization: Iie
    • Pronunciation: Ee-eh
    • Translation: No
    • Usage: Negative response.
